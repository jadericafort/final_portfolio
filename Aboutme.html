I am College Student



Hello, my name is Rosella Jade Asetre Ricafort. You can call me Jade or Rosella. Yes, I edit pictures and videos. This skill developed during the pandemic. I watched tutorials from TikTok and YouTube. After honing those skills, I put them to the test by uploading my own tutorials on TikTok and sharing my videos through my social media platforms.

My journey with coding began when I was a grade school student. My first experience was using Notepad and creating HTML. I found it enjoyable. I crafted my own website, experimenting with different backgrounds, including colors, images, and videos. I developed a passion for website design through this experience, which led me to pursue a course in computer science. I aspire to enhance my knowledge and skills further. I am eager to expand beyond website creation, delving into app and game development. My introduction to Python occurred during my work immersion at Ateneo de Naga University in senior high school (grade 12). As a college student, I am now learning C++, JavaScript, and deepening my understanding of HTML. My dream is to become a software developer, web developer, app developer, or game developer.
